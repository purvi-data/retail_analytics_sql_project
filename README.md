# Retail Sales Analytics Project

## Project Overview
This project analyzes retail sales data using SQL to uncover insights into customer behavior, product performance, and revenue trends.

The objective was to identify key revenue drivers, segment customers based on purchasing behavior, and evaluate category-level growth patterns to support business decision-making.

---

## Business Questions Addressed

- Which products generate the highest revenue?
- Which customer segment contributes the most to total revenue?
- How concentrated is revenue among top customers?
- Which category drives overall growth?
- What are the month-over-month revenue trends?

---

## Key Insights

- Top 10% of customers contribute a disproportionately high share of total revenue.
- Clothing is the highest revenue-generating category.
- A major revenue spike was observed in Month 6 (116% growth), largely driven by high-value customers.
- Product ID 51 generates the highest revenue despite lower units sold, indicating strong pricing power.
- Revenue fluctuations are influenced more by average selling price than transaction volume.
